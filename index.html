HEY THERE! WELCOME TO PETRODHAR

Mobile application designed to help Petrol pump dealers to manage their business operations efficiently.

The information is to outline the functional design and features of the Petrodhar mobile application, which is designed specifically for petrol pump dealers and their credit customers

The information covers different modules, functionalities, and interactions within the application. It explains the purpose and usage of various buttons and options available in the application.

The name "Petrodhar" encapsulates the essence of the application, combining the elements of "Petro" (pertaining to petrol) and "Udhar" (the act of borrowing) to reflect its purpose and functionality. Petrodhar, a mobile application, is specifically created for petrol pump dealers to effectively manage the records of their credit customers. These customers rely on the convenience of borrowing fuel and settling their bills within a specified period of time. With Petrodhar, petrol pump dealers can easily track and maintain accurate information about their credit customers, streamlining the credit management process for enhanced efficiency.
